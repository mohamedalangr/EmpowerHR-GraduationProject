# EmpowerHR User Guide

A short guide to help each role understand what to do first inside the system.

---

## 1) Candidate

**Main goal:** apply for jobs and track application progress.

### What you can do
- Browse open roles in the `Careers` area
- Submit your CV and application details
- Track your application history and updates
- Return later and continue from your candidate workspace

### Best practice
- Keep your profile details and CV updated before applying to new roles.

---

## 2) Employee

**Main goal:** manage daily work, requests, and self-service HR tasks.

### What you can do
- Review your `Dashboard` for priorities and updates
- Check `Attendance`, `Shifts`, and `Payroll`
- View `Reviews`, `Career Path`, `Training`, `Goals`, and `Tasks`
- Submit or monitor `Benefits`, `Expenses`, `Documents`, and `Support Tickets`
- Update your `Profile` and complete `Feedback` or policy acknowledgements

### Best practice
- Start from the dashboard each day, then use the sidebar for quick access to your most-used pages.

---

## 3) HR Manager

**Main goal:** run people operations, recruitment workflows, and employee services.

### What you can do
- Monitor workforce activity from the `HR Dashboard`
- Manage `Employees`, `Attendance`, `Payroll`, `Reviews`, `Training`, and `Succession`
- Handle `Approvals`, `Benefits`, `Expenses`, `Documents`, and `Tickets`
- Manage `Forms`, `Submissions`, `Job Postings`, and `CV Ranking`
- Use follow-up and watch panels to identify high-priority items faster

### Best practice
- Review dashboard signals first, then move to operational queues that show pending or overdue work.

---

## 4) Team Leader

**Main goal:** track your own work while keeping team execution aligned.

### What you can do
- Follow team goals and tasks
- Monitor recognition and team follow-up items
- Use personal employee pages for day-to-day self-service actions

---

## 5) Admin

**Main goal:** oversee access, governance, and system-wide operational health.

### What you can do
- Review users, approvals, and workspace coverage
- Access HR and leadership operational areas from the admin control center
- Monitor service queues and role-based workspace readiness

---

## Quick usage tips

- Use the left sidebar for grouped navigation
- Use favorites / recent pages for faster access
- Use `Login` with the role that matches your permissions
- Review alerts, watch cards, and follow-up sections regularly to avoid delays

---

## Support note

If a user cannot access a page, confirm that the account has the correct role and permissions for that workspace.
